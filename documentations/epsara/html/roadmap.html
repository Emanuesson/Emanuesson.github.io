Description of open work

The project is in an early state. Therefor this site is a list of all the features, that are planned for a release version. And further possible ideas for future problems are also collected on this place.

There will be different levels of stability for the API-functions. This is required, as I want to ensure that no problems by design will limit the applcability or functionality of the library. Api-stability: ver > 0.8 Api-changes only with announcement/Changelog: ver > 0.5
